SEO Mastery

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is integrated into the system instead of being handled as a separate checklist. The goal is for organic and paid traffic to support the same structure.

Methodology

  • Map queries to specific landing pages matching intent: informational, comparison, or purchase.
  • Build internal links across blogs, collections, and products to guide users deeper into the funnel.
  • Apply clean on-page structure: titles, meta descriptions, semantic headings, and FAQ sections.
  • Monitor Core Web Vitals, crawl errors, index coverage, and structured data.
  • Use Search Console to spot opportunities for new pages and refine existing ones.
