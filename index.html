Process

A complete cinematic build sequence

This is the real build flow. Each step is a deliverable that produces an output, feeds the next step, and reduces uncertainty.

Step 01

Evidence capture

Establish the truth on demand, competitors, and the conversion path before any build begins.

  • Demand and intent map from search behavior and market signals
  • Competitor breakdown: offers, pricing signals, proof patterns
  • Conversion path definition and friction inventory
Step 02

Positioning blueprint

Translate evidence into a clear market stance and offer structure that can scale.

  • Primary segment and secondary segments with exclusions
  • Differentiator stack and proof plan
  • Offer architecture and entry points
Step 03

Measurement spine

Build data reliability so every optimization and scale decision is safe.

  • Event design, UTMs, conversion goals
  • GA4 and GTM structure, deduplication, validation
  • Dashboards built for decision metrics
Step 04

Conversion environment

Design landing pages and flows that remove friction and compress time to decision.

  • Message match between ads, pages, and product content
  • Proof placement, FAQ structure, objection handling
  • Mobile clarity, speed hygiene, and navigation logic
Step 05

Content creation engine

Build content that supports performance with repeatable production rules.

  • Angle library and hook variants for testing
  • Creative briefs for static and video
  • Social pillars and calendar cadence
Step 06

Acquisition build and launch

Deploy campaigns as a structured system, not a collection of ad groups.

  • Search structure, negatives, match strategy
  • Shopping and Performance Max architecture
  • Retargeting and audience logic
Step 07

Launch control

Stabilize signals, protect learning, and validate tracking in real traffic.

  • Pacing and guardrails
  • Signal validation and anomaly checks
  • First optimization loop without breaking learning
Step 08

Stabilize and scale

Move from experimentation to predictability, then scale with rules.

  • Scaling thresholds and kill rules
  • Budget strategy tied to outcomes
  • Creative refresh pipeline and testing cadence
Step 09

Monitoring and continuous improvement

Keep the system alive with monitoring, reporting, and iteration loops.

  • Weekly and monthly operating rhythm
  • Dashboards that show what to stop and what to scale
  • Opportunity detection from search and customer behavior
