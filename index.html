SEO Brilliance and Methodology

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) work is treated as part of the system, not a separate checklist. The goal is to let paid and organic reinforce each other.

Methodology

  • Map search intent to specific landing pages instead of pushing everything to the homepage or one generic page.
  • Use internal linking between articles, collections, and product pages to support journeys for different intent levels.
  • Apply clear on-page structure: titles, meta descriptions, semantic headings, and FAQ sections.
  • Run regular technical checks on Core Web Vitals, crawl errors, sitemap coverage, and indexation.
  • Use Search Console queries to guide new page ideas and refine existing ones.

Impact on brands

  • TechnoMounts: SEO uplift grew organic impressions and clicks into the same landing pages used by paid campaigns, improving blended performance.
  • Haksoss CafÃ©: search work made the brand easier to find for high-intent local queries, supporting the loyalty and retention engine.
  • Rafiki and Mory Gym: SEO captured demand from people researching clinics and gyms, supporting the paid funnel instead of competing with it.
